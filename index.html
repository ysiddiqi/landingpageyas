Jewelery Trend 2020

Jewelry trends often include a particular style or material across various accessories. Whether on your ears or around your neck, accessories in the styles below will reflect trending jewelry in 2020. Past jewelry etiquette often frowned upon mixing silver and gold. In 2020, contrast is the key. Combine your own pieces or shop options that combine the metals for you for a guaranteed cohesive look. This trend has been on the rise for a few years because mixing contrasting metals will make your accessories more contemporary, put-together and eye-catching.
